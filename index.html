Our Discovery

Through building this website, we discovered that different recommender systems have distinct trade-offs. While collaborative filtering provides serendipity, it suffers from cold-start problems. Hybrid models can overcome many limitations but require careful design and higher maintenance costs. We also learned that ethical concerns like filter bubbles and privacy are not just theoretical — they directly impact user experience and trust.
