Real-World Large-Scale Recommender Systems

1. Amazon

"Customers who bought this also bought" — uses hybrid collaborative + content-based filtering.

2. Netflix

Personalized rows based on viewing history, ratings, and similar users (over 80% of content is discovered via recommendations).

3. Spotify

Discover Weekly & Daily Mix — combines collaborative filtering and audio feature analysis.

4. TikTok

Uses deep learning on short video engagement to predict what users will watch next.

5. YouTube

Next-video recommendation engine — one of the largest real-time recommender systems in the world.
