Ethical Issues

Major Ethical Concerns

1. Filter Bubble & Echo Chamber

Users only see content that matches their existing views, reducing diversity of thought.

2. Privacy Issues

Systems collect massive amounts of personal data to make accurate recommendations.

3. Bias and Fairness

Algorithms may reinforce gender, racial, or cultural biases present in training data.

4. Manipulation & Addiction

Designed to maximize engagement, sometimes at the cost of user well-being.
