Future Trends of Recommender Systems

1. Integration with Large Language Models (LLMs)

LLMs like GPT will generate personalized explanations and conversational recommendations.

2. Real-time & Context-aware Recommendations

Systems will consider location, time, mood, and current activity.

3. Privacy-preserving Techniques

Federated Learning and Differential Privacy will become standard.

4. Multimodal Recommendations

Combining text, image, video, and audio for richer suggestions.
